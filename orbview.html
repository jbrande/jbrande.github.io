Orbit Type: Ellipse

The LARGE GREEN DOT is the central object (the Sun).
The planetary orbit is drawn in RED when the orbit is in or above the XY plane and BLUE when the orbit is below the XY plane.
The LARGE RED DOT indicates the position of the planet along its orbit.
The YELLOW LINE is the Sun-Planet vector.
The MAGENTA LINE indicates the location of pericenter.
The CYAN LINE shows the ascending node of the orbit on the XY plane.
The YELLOW TRIANGLES point in the direction of orbital motion and are spaced at equal time intervals.
